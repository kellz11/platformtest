Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

Account data (email address, username, password hash), profile data you choose to add (display name, bio, avatar), content you post, community memberships, and technical data used for security (IP address and browser information at signup and sign-in, stored as hashes where possible).

Digital wallet

The platform may provision a Solana wallet associated with your account after email verification. We store only the public wallet address and a provider reference alongside your internal account ID. Private key material is held by the wallet provider's secure key-management system and never enters CORE's database, logs, or your browser. The public address may be used internally to allocate future rewards. It is never shown publicly unless you enable that in your settings. You are never required to fund the wallet or purchase cryptocurrency.

What is public

Your username, display name, bio, avatar, public posts and comments, and (unless you set your profile to private) the communities you follow. Your email address, login details, recovery information, wallet details, private settings, verification evidence, and moderation records are never displayed publicly.

Referral links

If you join through a community invitation link, we record which link brought you here so community managers can see aggregate onboarding statistics. Managers see counts, not your identity.

Retention & deletion

You may request account deletion via settings or by contacting us; content you posted may be anonymized rather than removed where needed for thread integrity.

Contact

Questions: reach the CORE team through the official community channels linked on the site.
